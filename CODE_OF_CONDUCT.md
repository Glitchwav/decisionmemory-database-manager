# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community.

Be constructive, assume good intent, respect privacy, and keep technical disagreement focused on evidence and outcomes. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and publication of private decision-making or credential data are not acceptable.

Report conduct concerns privately to `security@mnemox.ai`. Maintainers may edit or remove content, reject contributions, or restrict participation when necessary to protect the community.
